Convert between wavelength, frequency, wavenumber, and energy units.

    Features:
    * Real-time conversion between nm, GHz, cm⁻¹, eV, and kJ/mol
    * Comprehensive spectroscopy database 
    * Laser lines, atomic transitions, molecular spectra, calibration lamps
    * Visual color display
    * Completely offline - no internet required
    * No tracking, no ads, no permissions

Developed by the light & matter group at Leibniz University Hannover
for research and education in quantum optics and spectroscopy.
